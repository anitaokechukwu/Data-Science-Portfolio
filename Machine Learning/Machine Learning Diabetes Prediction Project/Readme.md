## Project Title
Predicting Diabetes Using Machine Learning

## Business Problem

** Healthcare providers want to predict whether a patient is likely to have diabetes based on:

* Pregnancies
* Glucose
* Blood Pressure
* Skin Thickness
* Insulin
* BMI
* Diabetes Pedigree Function
* Age
The goal is to identify high-risk patients early and support preventive care.

## Tools Used
* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Scikit-Learn
* Matplotlib

## Business Insights

* Finding 1
Glucose is the strongest predictor of diabetes.

* Finding 2
Patients with higher BMI are more likely to have diabetes.

* Finding 3
Age contributes significantly to diabetes risk.

* Finding 4
Family history increases diabetes likelihood

## Recommendations
1. Increase screening for patients with elevated glucose.
2. Prioritize obesity management programs.
3. Monitor older patients more closely.
4. Include family history in risk assessments.
5. Use predictive models to identify high-risk patients early.
