## Healthcare Diabetes Risk Analysis Using Python (Pandas)

## Business Problem
A healthcare facility wants to understand the factors 
associated with diabetes and identify high-risk patients for early intervention.

🛠 Tools & Libraries Used:

✅ Python
✅ Pandas
✅ NumPy
✅ Matplotlib
✅ Jupyter Notebook

## Key Findings
Glucose showed the strongest relationship with diabetes.
Higher BMI was associated with increased diabetes risk.
Older patients were more likely to have diabetes.
Family history contributed to diabetes occurrence.
Patients with both high BMI and high glucose represented the highest-risk group.

## Recommendations
Increase diabetes screening for patients with elevated glucose.
Promote weight-management programs.
Focus preventive care on older patients.
Include family history in risk assessment protocols.
Monitor patients exhibiting multiple risk factors.
